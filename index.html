ABOUT US


Starling Recycling provides a safe way to recycle your e-waste. Safety for your privacy and data is our main concern. We use advanced recovery methods to make sure your data is non recoverable. With your privacy in mind we refurbish each device

The E-Waste Problem electronic waste - Rhode Island Instituted a landfill ban in place in 2009 for all televisions, and computers (Read the statute: Rhode Island's E-Waste & Disposal Ban Law (R.I.G.L. Chapter 23-24.10). As our landfill continues grow larger and larger, the available space we have to bury our trash shrinks. Furthermore, e-waste can contain lead, mercury and other hazardous substances that pose a threat to human health and the environment if improperly disposed of at the end of their useful life.At Starling we are determined to give all technology has a home. From refurbishing computers to individual component level recycling we use every part of any equipment donated to Starling Recycling
